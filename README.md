# Funnel Vision

A mobile-first funnel builder for creators and small brands. Build a scroll-based sales funnel entirely from your phone in under 5 minutes — no designer, no developer, no desktop required.

## What it is

Funnel Vision turns your product video and copy into a hosted, shareable funnel that feels like TikTok. Full-viewport scroll-snap cards, a bounce effect that hooks visitors before they leave, and a direct path to your payment or signup link.

Drop the URL into your Instagram bio or ad. That's it.

## Why it's different

Every other funnel tool gives you a static page. Funnel Vision gives you a scroll experience with a video hook — the same mechanic that makes TikTok impossible to put down, applied to mobile commerce.

- Built entirely on mobile — no desktop step
- TikTok-style bounce effect on load pulls visitors into the funnel
- Full-viewport vertical video card as the hook
- Hosted instantly at a shareable URL
- Connects to your existing Stripe Payment Link or signup form

## Funnel structure

1. **Hero card** — headline, subheadline, background. The bounce fires here after 2.5s, peeking the video card below.
2. **Video card** — full-viewport vertical video (9:16). The scroll hook.
3. **Offer card** — product name, three benefit lines, CTA button (Buy now / Sign up) linking to your Stripe or signup URL. Trust line below the button.
